The Best Movies According to Yohan

My Top 3 Movies of All Time...

Good Will Hunting

A story about a young janitor at MIT who is a self-taught mathematical genius.

Pulp Fiction

A complex tale of crime and redemption, intertwining the lives of hitmen, a boxer, and a pair of armed robbers.

Crash

A thought-provoking exploration of racial tensions in Los Angeles, weaving together multiple storylines that intersect in unexpected ways.
